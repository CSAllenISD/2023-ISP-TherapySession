FAQs

Get answers to some common questions about our health questionnaire.

Here are some questions that are asked by most of our users:


What is the Health Questionnaire compared to other surveys?
The Health Questionnaire is a more personal survey compared to the generic questions that regular surveys provide, and we also provide fun facts for entertainment and helpful information for a specially selected answer, with helpful links and information

How does the Health Questionnaire personalize your answers? The Questionnaire uses Javascript based on the checkboxes you click for the selected answers and uses a special adding system to analyze your personal results to provide a specific outcome

Is the Questionnaire going to have bugs? Our team is working around the clock to provide you with the best possible experience, however a few bug might slip through the final product so please make sure to use our helpful feedback system to report any errors you find

Is the questionnaire anonymous? Will it store my results and answers? Yes, the questionnaire will keep all users anonymous for their privacy and not store or keep any data unless required by law or if you sign up for our website (coming soon)
